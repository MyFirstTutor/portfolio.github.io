About Anish Sir

Greetings! I am Anish Kumar, a Software Engineer driven by a profound passion for teaching. My journey in education commenced formally in 2013, and since then, I have dedicated over 14+ years to shaping the minds of students in Bhilai. What truly ignites my spirit is empowering students to unlock their potential, whether it's conquering intricate concepts in Organic Chemistry or navigating the captivating realm of programming.

I firmly believe in cultivating a profound understanding, steering clear of mere rote memorization. My aspiration is to make learning accessible, captivating, and pertinent for every student, equipping them not just for exams but for the challenges that lie ahead.

Grounded in logic and problem-solving due to my engineering background, I bring forth a distinctive amalgamation of analytical and conceptual teaching to my students, fueled by my love for Organic Chemistry. Join me in this journey of undefined discovery and growth.
